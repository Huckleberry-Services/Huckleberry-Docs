Huckleberry helps organizations track, understand, and improve the energy performance of their buildings and facilities. It brings together your utility bills, real-time equipment data, and building controls in one place so you can see costs, spot issues early, measure savings, and make better decisions.

These docs are written for the people who use the platform every day — facility managers, energy analysts, sustainability teams, and client administrators.

## What You Can Do with Huckleberry

- **See the big picture** — View costs, usage, and performance across all your sites in clear dashboards and charts.
- **Monitor your equipment** — Get live readings and smart alerts when something isn't performing as expected.
- **Track sustainability** — Understand your carbon and water footprint and generate reports for stakeholders.
- **Personalize your view** — Follow the sites that matter most to you and build a custom homepage with the information you need most often.
- **Access your building systems** — Connect directly to many building controls (BMS) for monitoring and adjustments.
- **Manage utility data** — Automatically pull in bills from providers or enter them manually, then analyze and export the results.
- **Prove savings** — Use measurement and verification tools to document energy improvements over time.
- **Stay informed** — Receive alerts and scheduled reports directly in Slack or Microsoft Teams.
