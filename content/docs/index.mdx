Huckleberry is a facility, energy, and environmental management platform that
helps organizations measure, manage, and optimize their building and utility
performance from a single data-agnostic system.

These docs cover how the platform works, how data flows through it, and how to
get the most out of each module. They&rsquo;re a living reference &mdash; check
back as new sections are added.

## What you&rsquo;ll find here

- **Concepts** &mdash; the building blocks of the platform: sites, meters,
  performance and comparison periods, normalization, and reporting.
- **Modules** &mdash; how to use utility billing, controls, environmental
  tracking, and reporting features.
- **Integrations** &mdash; how Huckleberry ingests utility data, building
  controls, and third-party APIs.
- **FAQ** &mdash; answers to common questions from operators, finance teams,
  and sustainability leads.
