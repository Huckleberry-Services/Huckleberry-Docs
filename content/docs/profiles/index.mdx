## Sites and Facility Profiles

Profiles give you a central place to manage information about your sites and equipment. This makes it easier to keep everything organized and consistent as your portfolio grows or changes.

In the interface you'll typically find:
- Searchable lists of your sites and equipment.
- The ability to export information for reporting or other tools.
- Tracking of key milestones or events at each location.
- Equipment details pulled together from your various data sources.

## Equipment Information

You can view and work with equipment across your facilities, including specifications and history. Having this information in one place helps maintenance teams and analysts work from the same data.

Huckleberry pulls equipment information from connected building systems (such as Skyspark and Niagara) as well as other records you maintain.

## Why Good Master Data Matters

Accurate site and equipment information improves the quality of everything else in the platform:
- More reliable dashboards and comparisons.
- Better context for alerts and fault detection.
- Cleaner reports and sustainability tracking.

See the Controls section for how equipment data appears in live monitoring and the Dashboards section for visualization options.
