## Sites & Profiles

Master data for facilities. Moving from legacy multi-section questionnaires to a modular Site Profile system (HCKLBRR-433).

- Grid and list views with search and export (Excel/CSV).
- Milestone timeline tracking per site.
- Equipment inventory aggregated from multiple sources (Skyspark, Niagara/NHaystack, database).
- Equipment specifications, change logs, and audit trails.
- Classification by equipment type and system type.
- Rules management: Skyspark analytics definitions with cost-to-fix analysis.

## Equipment & Tagging

- Multi-source equipment resolution.
- Haystack point mapping and semantic tagging vocabulary.
- Controls point mapping (JACE) with support for Numeric/Boolean/Enum/String points, inline creation, and cascading alarm config.

## Recent Enhancements

- Buildings foundation (HCKLBRR-935 series worktrees): per-building addresses, site foreign keys, CRUD support for finer-grained modeling.
- Equipment reference (equip_ref) recovery tooling and ambiguous group purging.
- Equipment tagging vocabulary and documentation.
