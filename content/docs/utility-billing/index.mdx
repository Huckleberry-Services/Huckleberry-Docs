---
title: Utility Billing
description: How Huckleberry ingests, normalizes, and analyzes utility bills.
---

The Utility Billing module turns monthly utility statements into structured,
analyzable data. Bills are ingested from a mix of provider APIs, broker feeds,
and manual uploads, then normalized into a common schema so cost and
consumption can be compared across sites, meters, and time periods.

## What it covers

- **Bill ingestion** &mdash; how statements get into the platform (Arcadia,
  Urjanet, direct provider integrations, manual entry).
- **Validation** &mdash; catching missing reads, anomalies, and provider
  errors before they pollute analytics.
- **Cost analytics** &mdash; baseline comparisons, weather normalization, and
  IPMVP-compliant measurement and verification.
- **Reporting** &mdash; recurring reports for finance teams and stakeholders.
