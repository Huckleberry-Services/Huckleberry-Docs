## July 2026 Release

We're excited to share meaningful improvements that help you monitor facilities more effectively, understand performance at a glance, and receive regular, useful insights without extra effort.

**Personalized Dashboards**  
Follow the sites that matter most to you and see a tailored homepage with quick summaries of utility costs, recent invoices, and equipment status. Rearrange and resize the information cards to match how you work, with your preferences saved automatically. Filter views across your selected sites for faster decision-making.

**Clearer Equipment Alerts and Diagnostics**  
Advanced Fault Detection (FDD) capabilities now provide easier-to-understand explanations of what went wrong with your equipment, including cause-and-effect details and how issues have progressed over time. Historical context for ongoing problems helps you prioritize maintenance and respond faster.

**Improved Facility Organization**  
Track buildings and equipment with greater detail, including better address information and clearer relationships between sites, meters, and systems. This makes reporting and navigation more accurate, especially for larger portfolios.

**Automated Reports and Easier Previews**  
Monthly energy performance summaries (including savings analysis) can now be scheduled for automatic email delivery to you and your stakeholders, with simple unsubscribe links. Previewing your data and reports has also been made more reliable.

**Stronger Building System Connections**  
nHaystack direct integration with Niagara BMS hardware provides more dependable live data access and controls compatibility. Equipment information and history are more consistent and complete without additional software layers.

**Enhanced Savings Tracking and Reporting**  
Continued refinements to tools that measure and verify energy savings, including automated report generation and better baseline comparisons to demonstrate results clearly.

**More Reliable Utility and Sustainability Insights**  
Improvements in how bills and usage data are processed lead to cleaner trends, better anomaly flagging, and higher-quality carbon, water, and cost reports you can confidently share.

For earlier updates, previous releases delivered automated collection of utility bills from major providers, comprehensive environmental footprint reports with easy-to-read charts and exports, foundations for personalized views, and overall improvements in platform speed and reliability.
