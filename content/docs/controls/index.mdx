title: Controls & Equipment
description: Connecting building controls and equipment data into Huckleberry.
---

The Controls & Equipment area lets you see what's happening inside your buildings in real time. It combines data from your building automation systems with the rest of your Huckleberry information so you can spot problems early and understand how equipment performance affects your energy costs and sustainability goals.

## What You'll See

- Live sensor readings and equipment status from connected systems.
- A clear list of your facilities with easy navigation to details.
- Equipment history so you can see how things have changed over time.
- Smart alerts when equipment isn't behaving normally.

## Advanced Fault Detection (FDD)

Huckleberry includes advanced fault detection that helps you identify equipment issues before they become expensive problems. You'll receive alerts with clearer explanations of what went wrong and how the issue has developed. This includes historical context for ongoing problems, making it easier to decide what needs attention first.

These capabilities work with both Skyspark and direct connections to your building systems.

## Direct Integration with Your Building Systems

Huckleberry supports strong compatibility with common building management systems, including Niagara hardware. This gives you more reliable live data and the ability to interact with your controls directly from within the platform.

## How It Helps You

- Understand the link between equipment behavior and your utility bills.
- Respond faster to issues with better context in the alerts.
- Reduce the need to log into multiple different systems to check status.
- Support maintenance teams with clearer information.

See the Dashboards section for how equipment status appears in your main views, and Profiles for managing your equipment inventory.
