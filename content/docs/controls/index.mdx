---
title: Controls
description: Connecting building controls and equipment data into Huckleberry.
---

The Controls module brings real-time and historical equipment data into the
platform alongside utility bills, so cost, consumption, and operational
performance can be analyzed together.

## What it covers

- **Connectivity** &mdash; supported protocols (BACnet, Modbus, Niagara,
  Skyspark, MQTT) and how data is mapped into the Huckleberry schema.
- **Equipment monitoring** &mdash; live meter and equipment readings, with
  fault detection and alerting.
- **Performance tracking** &mdash; equipment-level KPIs, heat maps, and
  maintenance signal generation.
- **Integrations** &mdash; how the controls layer pairs with Utility Billing
  for cause-and-effect analysis.
