## Measurement & Verification (M&V)

This area helps you demonstrate the results of energy improvement projects with credible, well-documented savings analysis. It is designed to support industry-standard approaches to measuring and verifying performance.

Key things you can expect:
- Regular automated reports (often called Report Cards) that summarize savings.
- Comparison of current performance against a baseline period.
- Tools to handle missing data periods so your analysis stays reliable.
- Ability to schedule reports for delivery to internal teams or external stakeholders, with easy unsubscribe options.

These capabilities are actively being strengthened to make it easier to produce the documentation needed for sustainability claims and incentive programs.

## Comparisons and Benchmarking

In the main dashboards you can rank and compare sites by cost, usage, and other metrics. This makes it simple to see which locations are performing well and which may need attention.

You can adjust the time periods being compared so the analysis reflects real operating conditions.

See the Dashboards section for more on rankings, trends, and heat maps.

## How Analytics Supports Your Goals

Good analytics turns raw utility and equipment data into actionable information:
- Spot trends and anomalies early.
- Support conversations with finance and leadership using clear visuals and exports.
- Build confidence in your energy improvement results with consistent reporting.
