Huckleberry's dashboards turn raw utility, equipment, and environmental data into actionable insights.

## Performance Dashboard

The primary hub for cost, usage, savings, and performance across all facilities.

Key capabilities:
- Monthly cost/usage trends with multi-utility breakdown (Electric, Gas, Water, Steam).
- Peak demand tracking.
- Cumulative energy savings visualization.
- Performance score calculation and tracking.
- Geographic heat maps and location ranking/comparison.
- Customizable, user-persistent chart layouts and saved filters.
- Prior-period comparisons with normalized "apples-to-apples" logic.

## Custom / "My" Dashboard

Personalized landing page. Users follow specific sites and see aggregated views without leaving the homepage.

- Global site filter (all followed sites or single-site drill-down).
- Widget Registry: extensible pallets for Utility Costs (stacked bars), Recent Invoices, Equipment Status (with 30-min freshness indicator).
- Drag-and-drop reordering with @dnd-kit, configurable sizing (half/full width, row height).
- Server-side persistence (config, followed sites, pallets stored in DB).
- Equipment detail modals with live Niagara readings.
- Promo banner on the main Performance Dashboard for discoverability.
- Admin support for managing per-client dashboards.

## Facility Management (Live View)

Real-time building monitoring combining Skyspark and Niagara JACE data.

- Live equipment status and sensor readings.
- Fault detection (Skyspark sparks + custom FDD rules) and Niagara alarms.
- Advanced FDD features (HCKLBRR-941): cause/effect narratives per rule, per-detector evidence, historical backfill for open faults, alarm lifecycle (active/ack/resolved) with batch state decisions.
- Alarm events table with severity, status, breach direction, filtering, and search.
- Dual-source support (Skyspark sites + Niagara buildings/JACEs).
- Site detail pages with images, coordinates, address.
- BMS/JACE proxy access for authenticated controls popups (see Controls section).

## Environmental Footprint

Carbon emissions and water usage analytics with sustainability tracking.

- Annual + trailing-12-month trends (dual-axis tons + tons/sqft).
- KPIs with prior-period arrows.
- Goal tracking (base year vs current with performance line).
- Month-aligned normalization for fair comparisons.
- UOM-aware conversions (CCF, CF, gallons, etc.) with automatic base-year detection.
- IQR-based invoice anomaly detection (with sensitivity control) — auto-excludes from aggregates.
- Missing-data banners for sites excluded from comparisons.
- Top performers (efficiency and reduction) with medal badges.
- Branded PDF exports for carbon and water (charts + KPIs + tables).
- Boosters & detractors reference tables.

## Comparisons

Portfolio-level ranking and side-by-side analysis.

- Rank sites by cost, usage, carbon, etc.
- Custom groupings (by group, state, or none).
- Configurable date ranges (performance vs comparison periods).
- Stacked bar visualizations.
- Note: Legacy Virtual Audit Comparison deprecated in favor of Site Profiles (HCKLBRR-433).
